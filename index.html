Who We Are

Indore's Most Trusted Event Decorator & Party Planner

AR Event Planner is Indore's premier event decoration and party planning company, dedicated to delivering creative, seamless, and memorable experiences. Located on Vishal Nagar Main Road, Rishi Nagar, Indore, our passionate team ensures every detail is curated to perfection.

From stunning weddings and engagement setups to birthday parties, children's parties, anniversaries, and custom theme decor — we transform your ideas into lifelong memories. Our creativity, punctuality, and commitment to innovative decor have earned us a perfect 5-star rating from our happy clients across Indore and central India.
